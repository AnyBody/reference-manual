AnyTransSphericalJoint¶

Type

Creatable

Parent class

AnySphericalJoint

Description¶

The AnyTransSphericalJoint is basically a spherical joint with one of the translational constraints removed. This makes it a ball-in-a-straight-rail.

The joint coordinates consist of three rotations similar to the spherical joint, but with an additional translational coordinate entered as the first element of the Pos vector.
